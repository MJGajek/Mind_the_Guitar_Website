Your guitar and your hands are only the instruments — your mind is what makes them speak.

Mind the Guitar is built for guitarists who want to move beyond dead shapes, memorized patterns, and mechanical drills, and start understanding what they play. The app connects music theory and the fretboard, turning intervals, chord construction, scale patterns, chord relationships, functional harmony, and more into meaningful tools you can use.

With over 40 focused lessons and more than 90 repeatable exercises, you can train your musical mind whenever you have a spare moment — even away from the guitar. So when you pick it up, you know what to play, where to find it, why it works, and how to express yourself musically.

Even in the most skillful hands, the guitar cannot express what the mind does not understand. That is why Mind the Guitar helps guitarists understand music theory so they can express themselves more freely.

To develop your musical mind, you do not always need a guitar in your hands. The app gives you exercises, visual explanations, and learning tools broken down into short, repeatable segments that you can practice whenever you have a few spare minutes — on the bus, on the train, during a lunch break, or anywhere else.

What you can practice

Mind the Guitar includes lessons about notes, intervals, scales, modes, chords, progressions, voice leading, functional harmony, and staff notation.

Included learning aids

Mind the Guitar includes a free tuner, metronome, and interactive circle of fifths. You can also unlock access to scale patterns for hundreds of scales and modes, thousands of chord shapes, and interactive visualizations for intervals, the circle of thirds, and modes.

Track your statistics

Mind the Guitar helps you track your statistics, so you can see your strengths and weaknesses as you learn.

Free and Plus content

The App offers free lessons and learning aids for beginner chapters. Mind the Guitar Plus unlocks full access to over 40 lessons, over 90 exercises, and all 10 learning aids.

Structured paths

Guided lessons and exercises that build up step by step — chord shapes, theory, rhythm and ear training.

Built-in tuner

Tune up without leaving the app. Microphone audio is processed locally — never recorded, never uploaded.

Local-only data

Your profile, progress and stats live only on your device. We don't run user accounts on our servers.

No tracking, no ads

No analytics SDKs, no ad identifiers, no profiling. Just the app and your practice.
